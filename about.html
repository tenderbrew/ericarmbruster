About

Engineer focused on reliable systems, clear tooling, and practical execution.

Background

I am a software engineer focused on backend services, automation, and infrastructure. Most of my growth came from shipping side projects, finding weak points, and hardening them over time.

I favor simple architectures with clear failure modes. Fewer moving parts usually leads to faster iteration and stronger reliability.

What I Do

I build product features and internal tooling that reduce friction for users and teams. My best work sits at the intersection of quality and delivery speed.

I prioritize strong interfaces, observable systems, and documentation that helps new contributors become effective quickly.

Outside of Work

Outside work, I spend time on games, film, and self-hosting. I run Nintendo Pipeline Club and enjoy building small communities around shared interests.

I also read economics because it sharpens how I think about incentives, risk, and long-term tradeoffs.
